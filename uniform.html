Uniform

At Barnhill, we believe that uniform plays an important part in unifying our pupils both together and with the school. We offer a range of official school uniform items in collaboration with BEUniforms and we allow some general formal attire. Please note that the terms "Boys" and "Girls' are included below to denote standard sizing conventions. We encourage our pupils to wear whatever they feel most comfortable in within our regulations. If you have any queries about uniform, feel free to contact us.
