Welcome to Barnhill Primary school

We are situated in the suburb of Broughty Ferry, Dundee. We have a roll of over 400 pupils in P1-7 with another 60 children who attend the nursery.

  • Our Aims Are:

  • To create a welcoming, safe and happy school where everyone is valued.
  • To promote a sense of responsibility, respect and caring attitude in all pupils and staff.
  • To provide a broad, stimulating education by learning through discovery, challenge and enjoyment to meet the needs of each individual pupil.
  • To ensure that the whole school community feel included and are proud of their work and achievements both in and out of school.
  • To instill confidence in pupils and staff to develop skills for learning, life and work.
  • To encourage all to reflect and self-evaluate as they strive to achieve their goals.
