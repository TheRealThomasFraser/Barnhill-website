Frequently Asked Questions

When does the school open and close?

The school is open from 09:00 to 15:15, Monday to Friday.

When are the pupil's break and lunch times?

Break is from 11:10 to 11:30
Lunch is from 13:15 to 14:00

Strikes or Snow days

In the case of the school being shut due to dangerous weather conditions or strikes, parents will recieve a text in the morning at 08:00. Children may continue to learn via the links on our Home Learning page if they so wish.
