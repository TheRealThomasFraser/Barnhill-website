Junior Leadership

Pupil Voice is extremely important in our school and we believe that the Junior Leadership Team allows our pupils to have an active role in the decision making process within the school as well as the learning and teaching process. Every class has their own representative voted by their peers and children within the school are encouraged to talk to their class reps about any issues of concern.
